about US

We are a group of motivated young people convinced to develop a startup that helps people to actually improve their lives. Our belief is that the strength of humanity is the human contact.
This ability is strongly reduced in the digital era where we spend more and more time watching screens than our own faces. We are trying to find the best motivation for people to put away their screens, encourage them to build their life in reality, not live in virtual world.

Experience a new way of spending time with your family and friends.

Reward yourself and your beloved ones with a wide range of rewards.

Contribute to the Sustainable Development Goal “Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.”
